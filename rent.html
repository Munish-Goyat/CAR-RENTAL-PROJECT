Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to rent a car?

You need a valid driving license, identity proof, and in some cases, a credit/debit card.

Is there any mileage limit on rented cars?

Yes, mileage limits vary by car. Extra charges apply for exceeding the limit.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before the booking time.

Are fuel charges included in the rental price?

No, fuel charges are not included. You need to return the car with the same fuel level.
