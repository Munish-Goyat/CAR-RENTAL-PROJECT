Rental Policies

Driver Eligibility

Must be 18+ years old with a valid driving license held for at least 1 year.

Original ID proof must be shown during pickup.

Fuel Policy

Cars are provided with a full tank and must be returned the same.

Shortages will be charged at ₹100/litre + GST.

Kilometer Limit

Limits vary from 120–360 km/day based on plan. ₹12/km after the limit.

Late Return

Late fee: ₹500/hour beyond scheduled time. 30-min grace allowed.

Cancellation

Full refund if canceled 24 hrs before trip. 50% refund within 24 hrs. No refund after trip begins.

Trip Modification

You can extend/shorten trips via dashboard. Depends on availability.

Vehicle Damage

All damages must be reported. Charges may apply based on extent of damage.

Cleanliness

Penalties from ₹500–₹2000 for excessive dirt or bad odors.

Smoking & Alcohol

Strictly prohibited. ₹1000 penalty if violated.
